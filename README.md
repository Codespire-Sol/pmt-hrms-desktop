## ⬇ Download

Grab the installer for your computer, then open it. Everything runs on your own PC — no accounts to buy, no cloud to set up.

| Your computer | Download | After you download |
|---------------|----------|--------------------|
| **Windows** (.exe) | [Download for Windows →](https://github.com/Codespire-Sol/pmt-hrms-desktop/releases/latest) | Double-click the **Setup** file and follow the prompts. |
| **macOS** (.dmg) | [Download for Mac →](https://github.com/Codespire-Sol/pmt-hrms-desktop/releases/latest) | Open the **.dmg**, drag the app to **Applications**, then **right-click → Open** the first time. |

> Installers are published automatically on each release. If you don't see a download yet, builds are published through our release pipeline — check the [Releases page](https://github.com/Codespire-Sol/pmt-hrms-desktop/releases).

## 🚀 Get started in 2 steps

No technical setup and no command line.

1. **Download & install** the app for your operating system (see [Download](#-download) above).
2. **Open it, create your admin account, and share the links** shown on screen with your team — they connect from their own browsers on the same office network. No install needed on their side.

Want more detail? See the step-by-step guides in [Documentation](#-documentation).
